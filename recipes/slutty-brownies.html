Slutty Brownies

Description

This dessert will become your all-time favorite after trying this recipe. The different layers of this dessert not only add size to your dessert but they also add a delicious mix of flavor. Travelling from cookie, to oreo, to brownie is quite the flavor adventure that is waiting to be explored by you.

Ingredients

  • Brownie mix
  • Cookie dough
  • Oreos
  • Egg
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Mix brownie mix with desired eggs, vegetable oil, and water to create brownie batter
  2. Line the bottom of pan with cookie dough
  3. Place a oreos on top of cookie dough in a grid-like pattern
  4. Pour the brownie batter into the pan covering the oreos and cookie dough
  5. Bake at 350o F for 35-45 minutes (place toothpick in the center to make sure the brownies are done)
  6. let cool for 10 minutes before slicing
