Chic-fil-a Pizza

Description

Chic-fil-a Pizza is a delicious combination of one of the best fast food restaraunts and one of the best types of food. From the chicken, to the waffle fries, to the Chic-fil-a sauce base, every bite of this pizza will provide a mouthful of amazing flavor.

Ingredients

  • Pillsbury pizza crust
  • Chic-fil-a sauce
  • Fried chicken tenders
  • Waffle fries
  • Shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Unroll pizza crust on a baking sheet and prebake at 425o F for 8 minutes
  2. After pizza crust is finished prebaking, add Chic-fil-a sauce to the crust to create the base of the pizza
  3. Cut the chicken into smaller bite sized pieces
  4. Add waffle fries and cut up chicken on top of the Chic-fil-a sauce base
  5. Add shredded cheese to the top of the pizza
  6. bake for another 6 minutes
  7. Let sit 2 minutes before cutting
