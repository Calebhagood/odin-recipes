Lasagna

Description

With this recipe of lasagna you will surely find yourself a new household weekly dish. This delicious lasagna will have you hooked because it excels in all the right areas. It's cheesy, meaty and will leave your belly extremely satisfied.

Ingredients

  • 1lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2lb italian sausage
  • Onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 2 Tbs. sugar
  • Parsley
  • Dried basil leaves
  • Salt
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fennel seeds
  • Black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Riccota cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef and italian sausage with chopped onion and garlic
  2. Mix meat with crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste and season to taste
  3. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water
  4. Mix riccota cheese with egg
  5. layer the lasagna as follows:
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Lasagna noodles
    3. Riccota cheese mixture
    4. Mozzarella cheese
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers and then top with parmesan cheese
  6. Cover with foil and bake at 375o F for 25 minutes
  7. Remove foil and let bake for another 25 minutes
  8. let lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving
